We all need feedback to know our impact and be and become our best. Regular feedback helps build awareness and accountability.

The Great Leader Survey provides leaders with a consistent and data-driven input for Great development and effectiveness conversations. The primary purpose of the Survey is to provide data-driven Insights to support identification of Focus Areas and selection of Development Actions. The timing is designed to inform the Q2 Check-In.

The Great Leader Survey complements the EOS feedback, providing specific and detailed feedback from a leaders direct reports, to help build awareness and accountability for ongoing development and impact.
