Keep it short
Your statements should be focused. If your V2MOM is too long, it may not be focused.

Words matter
Be thoughtful in your word choices. The words you choose make a difference.

Order is important
The order you list your Values, Methods, Measures and Obstacles shows their priority. When using your V2MOM to guide decisions, the order is key.

Make it your own
No two V2MOMs should be the same. From your Vision to your Measures the V2MOM should reflect your role and what you want to accomplish this year.
How to write a good V2MOM
The V2MOM is the way we align, prioritize and hold ourselves accountable for delivering each year. The V2MOM stands for:

Vision:
What do you want to accomplish? Your Vision should be specific to the contributions you and your team will make.

Values:
What beliefs and principles are important to you as pursue your vision? Values guide everyday decisions and tradeoffs.

Methods:
What are the key actions that you will take to achieve your vision?

Obstacles:
What is going to make accomplishing all of this difficult? What could stand in your way?

Measures:
For each Method, how will you know if youve been successful? Focus on the measurable outcomes versus activities.

In 1999, Marc Benioff created the vision for Salesforce and the plan to achieve it using a new management process he developed: the V2MOM, an acronym that stands for Vision, Values, Methods, Obstacles, and Measures.

History shows that it didnt take long for Marcs original vision to become a reality. And today, this tool (pronounced "V2 mom") is still at the heart of how we run our business. The V2MOM is our annual business planning and goal setting process -- and its the key to how as a company we align on our goals each year, prioritize whats most important, and hold ourselves accountable for delivering on what we set out to do. And the best part about it, everyone participates and we use it all year long in our 1:1s and team meetings to track our progress and celebrate our successes.

V2MOMs are created and published in Feb/Mar each year. Throughout the year review your V2MOM with your manager to maintain alignment and increase your chance of hitting your measures. Reviews and updates should occur on a quarterly basis at a minimum.
