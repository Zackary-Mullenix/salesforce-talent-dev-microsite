Leader Video on Check In four
During this conversation will review the V2MOM and Individual Development (IDP) to ensure that we are set up to finish the year strong. This means strong outcomes and impact, but also physically strong and personally well. We will have a real conversation about what support I need and how I will stay resilient as we close out the year. We will challenge ourselves to deliver our best work and impact.

We will be honest about what is realistic, prioritize what are the most important things to accomplish and where can we stretch further. We will review the most recent talent review feedback.

We recommend that you have this check in early in the quarter to give you every chance of finishing the year strong - both professionally and personally.

Quarter four
Leader Video on Check In three
During this conversation we will refresh the V2MOM and discuss impact to date. We will celebrate progress on development and identify what might be getting in the way to achieve growth and development. This is an important time of the year to connect on wellbeing and resilience, sharing honest two way feedback with each other on how we will drive impact through the rest of the year.

We will update my V2MOM and IDP post this conversation. We will make sure we are still aligned and update our V2MOM and IDP as required.

We recommend that you have this check-in midway through the quarter so you can review the full first half and ensure a great second half plan.
Quarter three
Leader Video on Check In two
During this conversation we will provide feedback to each other and review Individual Development Plan (IDP) actions to ensure success in achieving career and development aspirations. We will talk about feedback received from talent review conversations and survey feedback.

In this conversation we will review the V2MOM for the year, focusing on actions and obstacles. We will check the development actions will help overcome the obstacles identified in the V2MOM.

We will publish our V2MOM. We will check our V2MOM and ensure our Individual Development Plan (IDP) provides a clear and structured plan to support our success.

We recommend that you have this check-in late in the quarter so you can review the feedback from Talent Reviews and the Great Leader Survey.
Quarter TWO
Leader Video on Check In One
During this structured conversation we will reflect on our accomplishments achieved in the previous year, including lessons learned and opportunities to improve. We will also look at the coming year, aspirations and objectives. Together we will set our goals and have clear expectations of each other for the year.

We will close out last year’s V2MOM and set up your Individual Development Plan (IDP) for the new year.

We recommend that you have this check in as early in the quarter as possible so that it can inform your V2MOM.
